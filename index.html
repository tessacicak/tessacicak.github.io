--- layout: default title: "About" --- My name is Tessa Cicak. I am the Digital Materials and Collections Researcher for Liberal Arts Technologies and Innovation Services at the University of Minnesota. I help students faculty and staff with digital scholarship. I received my Ph.D in Biological Anthropology from the University of Minnesota in 2023. My research took me to museum collections across the globe.
